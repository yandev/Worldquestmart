COMPANY SUMMARY


BEULAH MEDIA LIMITED (BML) - Publishers of industry development issues, promotional schemes for businesses with strategic e-commerce and hard-tell publishing; amid other media marketing communication networks/material, is duly registered as a Limited Liability Company on a Five Million (5, 000, 000) Share Capital Base with the Nigeria Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in 2010 and currently with the Secretary of State in the United States of America on a One Million (1, 000, 000) Share Capital Base. Led by experienced management team of seasoned business executives comprising legal, financial and investment management experts, media and information technology analysts/reporters.BML is saddled with the responsibility to offer dependable news, features, analysis and incisive conferences towards profiling respective corporate businesses, prospective project ventures and individual countries; their investments opportunities, trade, travels and tours.

BEULAH Media Publications are designed to inform, educate, celebrate, suggest a way forward to would-be investors; profiling the best industry development practices from highly industrialized countries to the developing and under-developed nations, with destinations of businesses and investment opportunities at the large and small-scale levels for prospective entrepreneurs, captains of industries and the respective government departments, ministries, commissions and regulatory processes.

BEULAH Media Head Operations is currently networked from the United States of America witha major operation liaison network office proposed in every continentto oversee its projected operation network - USA (North America) | Nigeria(Africa) | UK(Europe) | UAE (Asia) | Brazil (SouthAmerica) | Australia (Australia) | Antarctica (Antarctica).

VISION To become an investor’s one-touch-catalogue that beams on industry development; developing and under-developed country’s trade, investments and travel opportunities.

MISSION To profile and promote positive landmark opportunities in areas of trade, investments and travels of diverse industrialized sectors, developing nations and under developed countries through electronicandprint media.

STRENGTH BEULAH Media is selectively managed by professionals, and strategically designates its operation through networking highly skilled capacities in the legal, financial, technical, managerial, commercial and didactic facets of all its daily operations. This array of professionalism prides the company’s output in excellence.

OBJECTIVES To positively add value to diverse industry sectors of various countries of interests through our widely circulated publications and electronic network.

To foster industry development with well-researched and promoted updates of our featured country’strade, investment, and travels opportunities through our publications and electronic network in order to attract prospective investors, potential entrepreneurs, captains of industries tourist and visitors with other pertinent governments across the globe, to patronize featured countries promoted quarterly though our electronic and print media channels.
